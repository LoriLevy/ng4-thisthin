Enhancers

Flavor enhancers are one of the best secrets for preparing low-fat meals!

Try these fat-free flavor enhancers to take flavor of your low-fat meals to a new level of YUM! They contain no high fructose corn syrup, msg, or additives that cause allergic reactions or your appetite to spike.

Please note: I do not sell these products. I am not affiliated with any company or product. I provide this information to help you select and consume foods you can enjoy, so you can easily shed fat and/or maintain your ideal weight. When you adopt a tasty, healthy lifestyle you will find it easy to stick with because you will know how to eat!

Products

Cranberry Relish

This is one of my favorite flavor enhancers. Stonewall Kitchens is (IMHO) the best. This is a fat-free flavor enhancer.

Suggestions:
  • Mayo replacement on a turkey or chicken sandwich
  • Use as a dip with pretzels for a savory snack
  • Use a dollop for topping for a yogurt snack
Stonewall Kitchens has lots of fat-free flavorful meal enhancers. Once you try some of them, you may find them hard to live without!

Primo's Gourmet Garlic Hickory Spice

I found this wonderful spice mix at a sportman's show and I order it online by the case from Primo's Gourmet Foods. Basically great on everything, it's my go-to spice and replaces salt and garlic powder in everything I cook.

Suggestions:
  • Sprinkle in egg white omelets
  • Season barbeque meats 2 hours before cooking
  • Shake into fat-free sour cream wth a heavy hand to create a very tasty dip

Peach Mango Salsa

I love the fresh sweet flavors of mango salsa! There are many brands available, but this one from La Mexicana is my favorite, and it is always fat-free. You can find it in these grocery stores and it has a long fridge shelf-life.

Suggestions:
  • Use as a salad dressing in salads
  • Use as a marinade for meats
  • Serve as a garnish for vegetable dishes
  • Top a baked potato with it
  • Use as a dip for Pretzel Thins or Special K Rice Crackers

Artisan Sea Salts

I got this as a gift from a friend that was visiting, and it has truly changed my life! Artisan Sea Salts add so much flavor! They are distributed by Fred Meyers Stores or you can order them on line. Shown here are the The Fume de Sel and the Salish, both in a fine grain.

Fresh Apples or Pears

Both apples and pears have lots of health benefits and eating them makes you feel full. The links will show you a visual reference of the many varieties. Buy 'em, slice 'em, dip 'em, dice 'em! They add a fresh delicious natural sweetness to any meal.

  • Dice and add to your salad, yogurt or oatmeal
  • Dip thin wedges into your home-made fat-free dips or yogurt
